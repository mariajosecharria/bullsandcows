Instructions

1. Start the game

In order to play you must choose a two-digit number, the digits cannot repeat and the number must be in the range 10-98. Numbers begginig with 0 will not be accepted.

2. Remember your number

You number will be placed in this box for you to remember it along the round. Your number cannot be changed throughout the game.

3. Required Inputs

You must insert the number of Bulls and Cows for the number proposed by the machine. As you will play with two-digit numbers the Bulls and Cows must be in the range of 0 to 2 inclusive. Additionally, you must propose a two-digit number in order to guess the machine´s number.

4. Results of your proposal

The number of Bulls and Cows in the number you proposed will be registered in the table below the required input spaces.

The game consists in guessing the other player´s number, consequently you will propose a number and the machine will answer with bulls and cows. Bulls correspond to the number of digits that are in the right position, while Cows correspond to the digits that are present in the number but are in the incorrect digit position.

Enjoy the game!

By María José Charria Macias
5. Send Button

When you are finished inserting the required and optional inputs and you are ready to continue, press the send button.

6. Optional inputs

In the user´s tools you will have a list of values that you can modify to remember the digits you can use. In order to eliminate digits from the list, insert each of them in one input space, the input must range from 0 to 9 inclusive. Additionally, you will find a draft card, which you can use to remember a digit. The draft card DON´T affect the proposed number.

7. World Ranking

Your place in the ranking, username and score will appear in the world ranking, your score will be updated every 3 games you play. The idea is to guess the opponent´s number as quick as possible, therefore, the less score you have, the better.
